About SDG 17

SDG 17 aims to strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development. It focuses on enhancing international cooperation, promoting trade,
and improving access to technology and financial resources to achieve the other Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Key Targets of SDG 17

Finance:

Mobilize financial resources for developing countries.

Technology:

Promote access to technology and innovation.

Capacity Building:

Enhance international support for capacity-building in developing countries.

Trade:

Promote a universal, rules-based, open, non-discriminatory, and equitable multilateral trading system.

Multi-stakeholder partnership:

Enhance global partnerships to support and implement sustainable development
